Privacy Policy

Your data, your story. We keep it simple and respectful.

What We Collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect what you share in the form—your email, role, and preferences—to help us understand who’s interested and how to build better.

  • Contact: email address
  • Profile: role, system, setup
  • Preferences: tools, pain points, feature priorities
  • Technical: browser user agent, submission timestamp

How We Use It

  • Send updates about Visionarium, beta access, and product news
  • Prioritize features and improve the experience
  • Maintain service reliability and prevent misuse

Storage

Data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud). It may be processed in the United States or other regions where Google operates.

Sharing

We don’t sell your data. We only share minimal information with trusted providers to manage the waitlist and send communications.

Retention

We keep waitlist data until you opt out or until it’s no longer needed for early access and feedback.

Your Rights

  • Access, update, export, or delete your data
  • Opt out of communications at any time

To make a request, contact us at privacy@visionarium.app.

Children

Visionarium is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as Visionarium evolves. We’ll post the latest version here with an updated date.
